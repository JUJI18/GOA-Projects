Dodge Charger(1969)

About Dodge Charger

The 1969 Dodge Charger is an iconic American muscle car, renowned for its aggressive styling and powerful performance. Part of the second generation of Chargers, it featured a distinctive front grille, hidden headlights, and a "Coke bottle" body shape. The most famous engine option was the 426 Hemi V8, although it was also available with the 440 Magnum. The Charger gained fame as the car driven by the Duke boys in The Dukes of Hazzard and continues to be a popular classic car.
